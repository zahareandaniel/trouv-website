Privacy Policy

Trouv Chauffeurs Limited · Company No. 14910070 · Last updated March 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Trouv, discretion is not just a service standard. It extends to every aspect of how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy sets out what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. Trouv Chauffeurs Limited (Company No. 14910070) is the data controller for all personal information collected through our website and booking channels.

2. What We Collect and Why

When you make a booking or enquiry with Trouv, we may collect the following information solely for the purpose of delivering your journey.

We collect your full name and contact details including email address and phone number. We collect journey details including collection address, destination, date and time. We collect flight number and arrival information for airport transfers. Payment details are processed entirely by Stripe and never stored by Trouv. We also collect corporate account and invoicing details where applicable.

We do not collect any data beyond what is necessary to provide your Transportation Service.

3. How Your Information Is Used

Your personal data is used exclusively to confirm and fulfil your booking, keep you informed about your journey, track flight arrivals for airport collection services, issue receipts, invoices and booking confirmations, and manage ongoing corporate account relationships.

We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Keeping Your Data Secure

We take the security of your personal information seriously. All data is stored securely with access restricted to authorised Trouv personnel only. Payment transactions are handled entirely by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment provider.

We comply fully with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

5. Sharing Your Information

Trouv does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your information may only be shared with your assigned chauffeur solely to facilitate your journey, for example your collection name and location, or where required by law or regulatory authority.

No third-party marketing, advertising, or data broking takes place.

6. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure it functions correctly and to improve your browsing experience. No tracking or advertising cookies are used without your consent. You may manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a data subject you have the following rights, which you may exercise at any time by contacting us.

  • You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • You have the right to have inaccurate data corrected.
  • You have the right to request erasure of your data.
  • You have the right to restrict or object to processing.
  • You have the right to data portability.

We will respond to all data rights requests within 30 days.

8. Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Booking records are typically retained for 6 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

9. Get in Touch

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us.
